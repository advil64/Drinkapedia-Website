What this app can do:

Alcohol

You can use this app to monitor your Blood Alcohol Concentration, and determine if you are fit to drive properly.

Water

You can determine how much water you should drink daily and how close you are to that water goal.

Caffeine

You can use this app to monitor how long you can stay awake with the amount of caffeine you have consumed.

Sugar

You can use this app to calculate if the amount of sugar you are consuming is healthy.
